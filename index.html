Common Knowledge

Electricity is the flow of electrical power or charge. It powers lights, devices, and modern life.

Electricity is commonly generated using turbines powered by wind, water, fossil fuels, or nuclear energy.

Access to electricity improves healthcare, education, and economic development worldwide.

Electricity News

New Solar Regulations Introduced

Monday — July 8, 2025

The government has released new policies supporting solar energy adoption in both residential and industrial sectors. The Ministry of Electricity and Water has initiated a nationwide campaign to educate citizens on reducing electricity waste. Tips include using smart thermostats, LED lighting, and optimizing appliance usage for a sustainable future.

Battery Breakthrough Powers Homes

Tuesday — July 9, 2025

Scientists have developed a new battery technology that offers twice the efficiency with half the weight. The breakthrough is expected to revolutionize electric vehicle range and solar energy storage. Early prototypes have shown promising results in both performance and environmental safety.

Wind Farms Expand Coastline

Wednesday — July 10, 2025

A major wind energy project has been launched along the coastline to increase power generation by 25%. The initiative is expected to create hundreds of new jobs in the renewable energy sector. Engineers plan to integrate smart grid technology for improved efficiency and real-time monitoring.

Electric Vehicles Hit New High

Thursday — July 11, 2025

EV sales hit a record this month, with infrastructure and affordability driving the market forward. Charging stations have expanded rapidly across urban and rural areas. New government subsidies are making electric vehicles more accessible to first-time buyers.
